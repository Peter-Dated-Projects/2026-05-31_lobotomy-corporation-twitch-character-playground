L3 in steering.py (pure functions, same style as separation_push, all same-surface via the HSEP_Y_BAND idea): add cohesion_pull (nudge toward mean x of same-band neighbours within CROWD_COH_RADIUS), alignment_nudge (nudge toward mean heading/velocity sign of same-band neighbours within CROWD_ALI_RADIUS, needs the new facing/velocity field), and local_density (same-band neighbour count within CROWD_DENSITY_RADIUS). Keep separation dominant. Add settings: CROWD_SEP_WEIGHT/COH_WEIGHT/ALI_WEIGHT, CROWD_COH_RADIUS/ALI_RADIUS/DENSITY_RADIUS, CROWD_MAX_NUDGE, CROWD_SLOWDOWN_PER_NEIGHBOR (commented block, mirror HSEP_*).

Perf: at N=100/12fps brute force is fine — do NOT add a spatial grid (the brief explains why; note the deferral trigger only).
