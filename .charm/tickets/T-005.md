## What to build -- `twitch_playground/render/speech.py`
- `load_robot_sprites() -> dict[str, pygame.Surface]`: load + convert_alpha the 4 PNGs (call AFTER display init, like the provider). Cache.
- `load_balloon() -> pygame.Surface`.
- `draw_speech(screen, *, character, text, font, anchor=...)`: draw the robot body for `character`, and above its head the balloon (balloon.png) with `text` word-wrapped inside the inner padding. Balloon is a plain coral-bordered rect, no tail (per KB). Scale the robot for the 1920x200 stage (settings.SCREEN_W/SCREEN_H; wide-short band, robot likely needs downscaling to ~200px height).
- Long messages: wrap to balloon width; cap lines + ellipsize on overflow.

## Constraints
- Do NOT edit settings.py, world.py, main.py, scene.py -- other tickets own them. Keep render/speech.py self-contained, called by the wiring ticket. Local module constants for sizes/colors (render/scene.py convention).
- Take all dynamic state as function args; hold no game state.
