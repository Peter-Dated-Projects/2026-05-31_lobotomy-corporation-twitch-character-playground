| [short-stage-jump-and-nameplate-headroom](short-stage-jump-and-nameplate-headroom.md) | On the 200px-tall stage two height budgets bind any level edit: jump apex (=JUMP_SPEED^2/2GRAVITY, ~96px) must exceed every tier gap, and a floating tier top must leave SPRITE_H+nameplate (~66px) above feet or the standing nameplate clips off the top. | current |
